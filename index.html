FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

HBOT is very safe when conducted by certified operators in a proper clinical setting. The most common side effect is mild ear discomfort during pressurisation, similar to what you feel on an aeroplane. Our technicians will guide you through equalisation techniques. Serious complications are extremely rare.
Most sessions last 60–90 minutes, including pressurisation and depressurisation time. You'll be breathing pure oxygen comfortably inside the chamber. Many clients read, listen to music, or simply rest during their session.
It depends on your condition and goals. A single session can help with acute conditions or sports recovery. Chronic conditions typically require 20–40 sessions for optimal results. We'll discuss a recommended protocol during your initial consultation.
A referral is not required for wellness sessions. For medical HBOT (treatment of specific conditions), we recommend a consultation with your doctor and our clinical team first. We work alongside your existing healthcare providers.
